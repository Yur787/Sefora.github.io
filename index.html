Progression4 Trees

Each path has its own mastery curve. Progress is earned — never given.

🍎 Devil Fruit Progression
StageNameDescription
1Awakening SeedFruit consumed. Powers accessible but unrefined. High stamina cost on all abilities.
2Control Core techniques unlocked. Elemental transformation stable (Logia). Forms reliable.
3Mastery Full moveset accessible. Haki synergy begins. Stamina cost greatly reduced.
4Awakening Rare threshold. Affects environment, not just body. Transforms combat fundamentally.
5True Form Mythic Zoans only. Identity fully unlocked. Power scales with imagination.
⚡ Haki Progression
StageNameDescription
1Dormant Exists but cannot be consciously activated. Occasional instinctive bursts only.
2Awakened Conscious activation. Basic armament coating or presence detection online.
3Trained Reliable in combat. Armament penetration of Logia. Observation aura sensing.
4Advanced Ryou (internal) for Armament. Future Sight for Observation. Burst for Conqueror.
5Supreme Conqueror coating unlocked. Island-range Observation. Ryou through weapons and terrain.
👊 Martial Arts Progression
StageNameDescription
1Novice Basic strikes. No style-specific techniques unlocked yet.
2PractitionerCore style moves available. Footwork and timing improve significantly.
3Expert Full technique set unlocked. Style-specific enhancements online.
4Master Haki integration. Signature finishing moves unlocked.
5Grandmaster Style transcended. Unique personal techniques. Can teach others.
🗡️ Swordsmanship Progression
StageNameDescription
1Untrained Basic swings. Cannot control blade momentum or channel intent.
2Student Fundamentals solid. Standard cuts and parries reliable.
3Skilled Style-specific moves online. Aerial and multi-hit combos available.
4Elite Haki-infused strikes. Pressure waves. High-grade sword tamed.
5World's FinestAbsolute pinnacle. One per era. Strikes that cut nothing and everything.
